<h2>ğŸš© Problem Statement</h2>

<p>
A subscription-based digital platform wants to understand:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>How customers are distributed across countries, genders, and subscription plans</li>
  <li>Which subscription plans perform well and which are underperforming</li>
  <li>Which user groups show high churn or low retention</li>
  <li>How revenue is generated and how it varies across plans and payment methods</li>
  <li>How users engage with the platform across different devices</li>
  <li>How metrics like DAU, MAU, screen time, and sessions reflect overall platform stickiness</li>
</ul>

<p>
The goal is to build a unified analytics dashboard that helps stakeholders track 
<b>customer growth, churn patterns, revenue trends, and engagement behaviors</b>, 
ultimately improving decision-making and product strategies.
</p>
